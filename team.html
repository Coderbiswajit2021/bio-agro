Our Team

MR. SIBA NARAYAN SAMANTARAY

Managing Director & CEO

Mr. Siba Narayan Samantaray is the Managing Director of Bio Agro Energy Private Limited, a Graduate Engineer with MBA in Finance & Personnel Management and also a Law Graduate. Holds more than 32 years experience in Distillery plants and other related plants.

MR. PURANDARA BABU POTHUGUNTA

Director

Mr. Purandara Babu Pothugunta is the Director of Bio Agro Energy Private Limited. He pursued a Master’s Degree in Public Administration highlighting his dedication to gaining expertise in the realm of Public Governance and administration. With over three decades of expertise in Public Administration, Hydro Power Companies and the import of Aluminum Easy Open Ends for packaging, he possess a wealth of invaluable Industry knowledge.

MR. ANUPAM PATNAIK

Director

Mr. Anupam Patnaik is the Director of Bio Agro Energy Private Limited. He earned his Bachelor's Degree from Symbiosis International University in Pune and his Master's degree in International marketing from Leeds University. With over a decade of diverse expertise spanning the automobile industry, film production and media & advertising, his journey began in 2011 within the dynamic realm of the Automobile Sector. He now hold the prestigious position of Director in two prominent automobile companies, specializing in Audi and Chevrolet dealership.
